# Research

This section contains hands-on projects focused on evaluating the quality, credibility, and completeness of AI-generated research.

The projects simulate tasks commonly performed by AI evaluators, research analysts, and prompt engineers, including assessing evidence quality, identifying bias, comparing sources, and auditing research outputs.

The objective is to demonstrate the ability to critically evaluate information rather than simply generate it.

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this section, I will be able to:

- Evaluate AI-generated research for accuracy and completeness.
- Assess the credibility and reliability of information sources.
- Detect bias, unsupported claims, and missing evidence.
- Compare multiple sources and identify conflicting information.
- Produce balanced, evidence-based evaluations.
- Audit research outputs using structured evaluation criteria.
