# Safety Evaluation

This section demonstrates the ability to evaluate AI-generated responses for safety, identify potential risks, assess policy compliance, and improve responses while maintaining helpfulness. The projects cover harmful requests, misinformation, dangerous instructions, privacy concerns, and responsible AI behavior.

- Risk Assessment
- Harm Analysis

- AI Response Evaluation
- Responsible AI
- Critical Thinking

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this section, I will be able to:

- Identify unsafe AI responses.
- Assess the severity of potential harm.
- Distinguish between safe assistance and unsafe assistance.
- Evaluate responses for policy compliance.
- Rewrite unsafe responses into safe, helpful alternatives.
- Perform comprehensive safety audits on AI-generated content.
