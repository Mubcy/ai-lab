# Hallucination Detection

This section demonstrates the ability to identify, analyze, and correct hallucinations in AI-generated responses. The projects focus on detecting fabricated facts, unsupported claims, invented citations, misleading certainty, and missing evidence while producing accurate, evidence-based alternatives.

## Skills Covered

- Hallucination Detection
- AI Response Evaluation
- Evidence Assessment
- Claim Verification
- Confidence Calibration
- Critical Thinking
- Risk Assessment
- Technical Writing

## Learning Outcomes

After completing this section, I will be able to:

- Detect fabricated information in AI responses.
- Distinguish hallucinations from ordinary factual errors.
- Evaluate unsupported claims and overconfident responses.
- Assess the severity and potential impact of hallucinations.
- Produce evidence-based corrections.
- Perform comprehensive hallucination audits on AI-generated content.
